Disgusting coffee

Description

Regular coffee, just a normal size, a normal amount of coffee and just a normal coffee.

Ingredients

  • 300ml water
  • Kettle
  • 5-10g ground coffee
  • French press
  • Mug

Steps

  1. Boil the water in the kettle.
  2. Place ground coffee in to the french press.
  3. Once the water is boiled poor over the coffee, but don't press the plunger.
  4. Let the coffee steep for 5 minutes (tip: put the lid on top and push the
    plunger so that the mesh is just above the water, to retain maximum amount of heat).
  5. After 5 minutes take a spoon and stir the mixture and wait final 10 minutes.
  6. Push the plunger so that the mesh filter is just submerged in the water (about half
    of centimeter below water).
  7. Poor coffee in to a mug while keeping an eye on carafe so that the black sludge doesn't
    come out in to the drinking vessel.
  8. Enjoy the black coffee without milk/cream/ice cream/sugar/whitener/sweetener.
