Oats and seeds

Description

Soaked oats and seeds is good for you, eat this at least once a day to keep yourself strong.

Ingredients

  • For the base:
    • 2 spoonfuls of chia seeds
    • 2 spoonfuls of chia flax seeds
    • 1 spoonfuls of black sesame seeds
    • 3-4 spoonfuls of whole grain oats
    • Good quality water
  • Optional:
    • 2 spoonfuls of raisins
    • Half of spoon cocoa powder
    • Handful of nuts
    • Handful dried fruit
  • Optional after soaking:
    • Fresh berries (blueberries or strawberries)
    • Fresh fruit (apple, banana, pear, orange)

Steps

  1. Place the base and if using the optional ingredients either
    in to a mason jar or a bowl.
  2. Cover with water and stir (everything has to be submerged
    and there should be some water extra, because chia seeds and oats
    soak up water really well).
  3. Cover the jar or plate with a lid or a small plate and
    put it in to the refrigerator (tip: warn other people that are using
    the fridge to be careful and not to tip over the covered bowl or jar).
  4. The soupy cold porridge is ready to be eaten in an hour, but
    leaving over night leaves better result.
  5. If using fresh fruits/berries, chop up fruits in to bite size
    pieces and lay them on top of the soaked oats.
